You're Ybba (Cloud) and you have to defeat Abby (Frisk). In order to do that you've got to take actions. If you attack, you inflict damage to frisk, you may deal critical hits (if the screen turns yellow you or your opponent did a critical hit). You may heal. Useful when you have low hp, healing consumes 30 MP. If you want to see your stats, then hit the player stats button. You can see Abby's name change color, that can tell you many things, be sure to pay attention to that. Once you have chosen what to do, hit the next button to let the battle continue.
